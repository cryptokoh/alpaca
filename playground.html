Welcome to the Playground

Interact with the 3D alpaca or upload your own models for a quick preview.

Controls

  • Click the alpaca for a rainbow burst
  • Drag to rotate the model manually
  • Scroll to zoom in the model viewer
  • Tap on mobile for the burst effect

Supported Formats

  • .STL — Standard tessellation language
  • .OBJ — Wavefront object format

Tips

  • Use the Customizer tab to change alpaca colors
  • Toggle accessories like hats and sunglasses
  • Upload an .stl file to preview it in 3D
  • Take a screenshot of your custom alpaca
